## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Is this actually free? What's the catch?
**A1:** Yes, completely free. No credit card, no login required to run your audit. We show you the full results upfront. If your audit surfaces significant savings (over $500/mo), we'll mention Credex — our service that can get you discounted AI credits. You're never obligated to use it.

### Q2: Is my workspace and subscription data private?
**A2:** Yes. All personally identifiable information (PII)—including work emails and company names—is securely stripped on the client side before saving. Publicly shared dashboard URLs display fully scrubbed, anonymous cost telemetry, ensuring absolute privacy.

### Q3: Does this support custom API consumption billing?
**A3:** Yes. The audit engine parses volume thresholds for OpenAI, Anthropic, and Gemini API direct usages, automatically calculating if your API spends qualify for direct bulk consolidation credits.

### Q4: Our stack isn't listed. Can we still use it?
**A4:** The audit currently covers the 8 most common AI tools for dev teams: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. If your primary tools aren't listed, the audit will still run on what you do use. We're adding tools based on what users request — there's a field to suggest what's missing.

### Q5: How is this different from just reading the pricing pages myself?
**A5:** Three things the pricing pages don't tell you: whether your plan fits your team size, whether two tools you're paying for overlap in capability, and what a team like yours typically spends. This audit runs all three checks simultaneously and gives you a specific recommendation with the math shown — not a generic "you could save money" nudge.
